## Overview

**Porsenigama 2022** (*Pekan Olahraga dan Seni Gadjah Mada*) is a UGM-organized sports and arts competition website. My role in this project was as **UI/UX Designer**, responsible for the complete product design lifecycle — from discovery and user research through to the final high-fidelity handoff.

Working within a 7-person cross-functional team (developers, PM, and the organizing committee), I designed the full interface in **Figma**, covering 5 core pages: Information, FAQ, Match Schedules, Standings, and Gallery. The design addressed the primary pain point of navigating complex sports schedules and medal standings, replacing a manual/document-based process with an intuitive digital platform.

A **reusable design system** (typography, color tokens, and component library) was also delivered, enabling the development team to ship UI 30% faster and maintain visual consistency across the site.

## Tech Stack & Key Competencies

**Design Tool:** Figma (End-to-end Design, Prototyping, Design System)

**Deliverables:** User Flows, Wireframes, Lo-fi & Hi-fi Mockups, Component Library

**Implementation:** HTML, CSS, React.js, Tailwind CSS (by dev team)
