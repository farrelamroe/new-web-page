## Overview

**iGEM UGM** is the official competition wiki website for UGM's team in the **International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM)** competition — the world's largest synthetic biology competition, judged internationally in Paris, France. Built solo within a 3-person engineering team, the wiki served as the team's primary presentation platform for judges and the global scientific community.

The project ran across **3 consecutive competition cycles (2023–2025)**, evolving each year with new content, improved UX, and higher performance standards. The website directly contributed to the team securing multiple prestigious awards across all cycles.

**Awards:**
- 🥇 Gold Medal, Inclusivity Award, Top 5 Best Diagnostics Project — iGEM 2023
- 🥇 Gold Medal, Grand Prize Winner, Best Presentation, Best Website — BIOS 2024
- 🥇 Gold Medal — iGEM 2025

## Tech Stack & Key Competencies

**Framework:** Next.js (SSR, Static Generation)

**Styling:** Tailwind CSS, Fully Responsive Design

**Version Control:** GitLab (iGEM's official repository platform)

**Content:** Scientific data synthesis from Lab, Wet Lab, Dry Lab, and Human Practices teams
