## Overview

**Fake News Detection System** is an undergraduate thesis project that developed an automated fake news detection model for Indonesian-language news articles using Deep Learning. The research implemented and compared two Bidirectional architectures — **BiLSTM (Bidirectional Long Short-Term Memory)** and **BiGRU (Bidirectional Gated Recurrent Unit)** — to capture complex linguistic patterns in both forward and backward directions through text sequences.

The study adopted a **weakly supervised learning approach**, using TF-IDF for textual feature extraction. The BiLSTM model achieved the highest performance with **78% accuracy and 0.85 precision**, successfully outperforming traditional SVM baselines in capturing nuanced semantic relationships and context within Indonesian news content.

## Tech Stack & Key Competencies

**Language & Frameworks:** Python, TensorFlow, Keras, Scikit-learn

**NLP & Feature Engineering:** TF-IDF Vectorization, Text Preprocessing, Tokenization

**Deep Learning Models:** BiLSTM, BiGRU (Bidirectional Sequence Modeling)

**Methodology:** Weakly Supervised Learning, Binary Classification, Cross-Validation
